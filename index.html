Authorized Plywood & Interior Fittings Distributor in Hyderabad

Welcome to Pakshal Agencies, your trusted partner for premium building and interior design materials. Based in Goshamahal, Hyderabad, Telangana, we are the leading distributor and supply partner for high-quality plywood, decorative laminates, wood veneers, and state-of-the-art architectural hardware fittings. Since 2011, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and authenticity, serving architects, builders, B2B dealers, and interior designers across South India.

Our core range of plywood products features Boiling Water Proof (BWP) marine grade sheets, double-pressed calibrated plywood, high-density blockboards, and flush doors. Made from selected Gurjan timber, our plywood brands like Royal Club, Marine Club, and Gurjan Club provide unrivaled structural integrity, termite-proof treatment, and borer resistance. These materials are engineered for heavy load-bearing frames, modular wardrobe systems, and premium modular kitchens.

For decorative surface finishes, we distribute a rich catalog of wood veneers, high-pressure laminates, and high-gloss acrylic panels. We offer Italian-inspired Armani acrylic sheets, Indigo matt-finish laminates, and natural woodgrain textures. These premium finishes provide high scratch resistance, anti-fingerprint technology, and UV stability, making them perfect for luxury TV consoles, executive desks, custom joinery, and slatted wall paneling.

Our architectural hardware division supplies precision cabinet hinges, hydraulic soft-close hinges, sliding drawer channels, tandem boxes, and designer pull handles. As an authorized distributor, we bring you genuine hardware fittings from globally leading brands including Ebco, Hettich, Godrej, Hafele, and Dorset.
